Module S6: Meta-Awareness

Key Question: How do I become aware of my awareness itself?

Script:

Meta-awareness is being aware of the changes in your consciousness.

Go to a spa. Get a Thai massage. Notice the before and after.

Go for a mountain hike. Notice how you feel at the bottom versus the top.

Enter a meeting with stressed colleagues. Notice how your state shifts.
