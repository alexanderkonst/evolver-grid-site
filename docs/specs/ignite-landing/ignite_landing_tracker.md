### [x] 1.1 Master Result

> **From** "I have a gift I can't name, a business I can't see, and no idea where to start"
> **To** "I see my craft, my business, and my first paying client — on one page"

**The landing page's Master Result is NOT the session's Master Result.**

The *session* transforms the founder.
The *landing page* transforms the **visitor into a buyer.**

So the landing page's ACTUAL Master Result is:

> **From** "Interested stranger who suspects they need this but isn't sure"
> **To** "Person who's paid $555 and booked their session"

**Point A (Before landing page):**
- Arrived via DM, link, referral
- Curious but skeptical
- "Is this for me?"
- "Is this person legit?"
- "Is $555 worth it?"

**Point B (After landing page):**
- "That headline describes ME"
- "These people had MY experience"
- "I can see exactly what I get"
- "$555 is nothing for THIS"
- Clicks → pays → books
