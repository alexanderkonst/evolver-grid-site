# The Roasting Protocol
## Iterative Adversarial Refinement for High-Fidelity Artifacts

> *"Don't fix. First, see clearly. Roast until the truth is undeniable. Only then — refine."*

---

## The Heart of the Matter

The Roasting Protocol is a **disciplined restraint technique**. The instinct to fix immediately is suspended in favor of seeing the full picture first. By roasting (critiquing) an artifact multiple times WITHOUT fixing, you:

1. **Surface hidden assumptions** that would remain invisible during incremental fixes
2. **Build activation energy** for a transformative leap rather than incremental improvement
3. **Allow the subconscious to synthesize** while the conscious mind catalogs problems
4. **Prevent premature convergence** on a local maximum

---

## The Protocol (5 Steps)

### Step 1: First Roast
Critique the artifact ruthlessly. Find every weakness, gap, vagueness, assumption.
**Rule:** NO FIXING. Only observe and articulate.

### Step 2: Continue Roast
Go deeper. What did the first roast miss? What patterns emerge?
**Rule:** HOLD YOUR HORSES. Still no fixing.

### Step 3: Meta Roast
Roast the roast itself. What did the critique miss? What biases are in the critique?
**Rule:** STILL NO FIXING. Just seeing.

### Step 4: Synthesized Iteration
Now — and only now — create the next iteration. Incorporate EVERYTHING the roast unearthed.

### Step 5: Repeat 3x Total
Run steps 1-4 three complete cycles. Each cycle reveals new depths.
