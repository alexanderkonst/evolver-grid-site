# Worktools Evolution Holomap

**CollabTech Holomap — Taxonomy Across Maturity Stages — v1.0**

Sasha's original, co-created with AI using integrative approaches. Source PDF in `assets/`.

The evolutionary stages are not linear — they are spiralic. We loop through them at new octaves for each creation.

## The map

| Stage | Name | Essence of What Happens | Qualifier |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Analog Coordination | We coordinate by talking and paper; sharing is manual and slow. | Most work units live offline or in people's heads. |
| 2 | Digital Files | Work is in docs and email; online but disconnected. | Most work units are docs/emails with no consistent link to owners or tasks. |
| 3 | Work in the Cloud | We co-edit in cloud docs/boards/chat, but work is scattered across tools. | Most work units are co-edited online; tasks/files/conversations are not consistently linked. |
| 4 | Linked Work | Tasks, files, people, and decisions connect on one work map; dependencies and status are clear. | Most tasks show owner + linked files + last decision in one place; cycle time clearly improves. |
| 5 | CollabTech | Small, flexible teams (people + AI) add externals fast, agree with one click, do the work on the same map, and get auto-settled with fair splits. | An external collaborator can be added in <15 min, click-agree inside the integrated tool, and payments auto-split on delivery (24-48h). |
| 6 | Programmed Networks | Work routes by clear rules, money streams as progress happens, teams spin up on demand. | Policies (not meetings) trigger most hand-offs and payouts; progress-based streaming payouts are live; disputes close fast by a standard process. |

## ❤️ Essence

This map tracks how mature a group's *tooling* is, from talking and paper (Stage 1) to a fully programmed network where policies trigger payouts automatically (Stage 6). It's separate from the Collaboration Spectrum, which tracks human readiness — this one tracks the tech underneath.

## 🧠 Significance

The qualifier row is the diagnostic. It's not "which tools do you use" — it's a testable claim ("can an external be added in under 15 minutes and get paid within 48 hours?"). That makes the stage checkable against reality, not just a label a team picks for itself.

## 🔥 Consequences

Most of the world today sits at Stage 3-4: cloud docs and boards, maybe a linked work map, but no real agreement or payment automation. The venture is building for Stage 5-6 — CollabTech and Programmed Networks — where teams (people plus AI) form fast, agree with one click, and get paid automatically as work lands. That's a two-stage jump ahead of where most tools currently operate, which is the gap the platform is built to close.
