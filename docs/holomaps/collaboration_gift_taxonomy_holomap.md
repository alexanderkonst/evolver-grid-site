# Collaboration Gift Taxonomy — v1.0 (Day 120, July 10, 2026)

Co-created by Sasha + AI, Day 120, by reverse-engineering one sentence: "this AI introduced me to exactly the right person." What actually passed between the two people, broken into parts.

The question it answers: when an introduction was "exactly right," what actually passed between the two people? Seven aspects. Aspects 1-5 are the single gifts (a menu, not a ladder — any can occur at any relationship stage). Aspect 6 is the Mi-Fa shock: gifts compound into a standing bond. Aspect 7 is the Si-Do shock: the next octave — the relationship becomes its own holon. Each aspect opens into a Heart / Mind / Gut trinity (what it did to your being / your understanding / your action).

**1. MIRROR — they revealed you to yourself** (I · interior)
- ❤️ Recognition — they saw your essence; you felt met at the level you actually operate.
- 🧠 Blind spot — they named what you couldn't see from inside.
- 🔥 Exact words — they handed you language you could immediately decide and act with.

**2. COMPASS — they oriented you** (It · knowledge)
- ❤️ Permission — "you're allowed to want that / skip that" — a felt release of a false constraint.
- 🧠 Map — a framework or distinction that reorganized the whole terrain.
- 🔥 Timing — the concrete next move, and when: now, or not yet.

**3. DOOR — they gave you access** (Its · systems)
- ❤️ Vouching — their reputation transferred to you; you were trusted on arrival.
- 🧠 Decoding — they explained how the room actually works (unwritten rules, real players).
- 🔥 Entry — the third-party intro, the invitation, the deal itself.

**4. CO-CREATION — they built with you** (We · joint action)
- ❤️ Trust — someone committed their hours to your thing; skin in the game.
- 🧠 Complement — their genius covered your gap (and yours theirs) — the exchange itself taught you.
- 🔥 Shipped work — something exists in the world that neither could have made alone.

**5. MOTIVATION — they energized you** (Heart-field · animation)
- ❤️ Kinship — "I'm not alone / not crazy" — the loneliness of the edge broke.
- 🧠 Believed future — they described your success so concretely it became plausible to you.
- 🔥 Challenge — a provocation that mobilized you; you moved because they dared you.

**6. WEAVE — the gifts compound into a named relationship** (Mi-Fa shock: single gifts → sustained bond)
- ❤️ Champion — Door + Motivation held over time: they advocate for you in rooms you're not in.
- 🧠 Mentor — Mirror + Compass held over time: they see you AND orient you, repeatedly.
- 🔥 Partner — Co-Creation + Compass held over time: you build and steer together.

**7. COMMUNION — the relationship becomes its own holon** (Si-Do shock, next octave)
The invariant that changes across the whole taxonomy is WHO ACTS. In gifts 1-5 the other person acts on you. At 6 the bond acts — the standing relationship produces value on its own rhythm. At 7 the relationship itself becomes the actor: a third being with its own name, mind, and metabolism.
- ❤️ One name — outsiders address you as a unit ("ask those two"); you defend the bond the way you defend yourself.
- 🧠 One mind — ideas arise between you that neither can trace to themselves; your private vocabulary does real work.
- 🔥 One metabolism — money, opportunities, and risk flow through shared channels without per-instance negotiation; one balance sheet of fate.

MECE note: aspects 1-5 are complete for MOMENTS; 6-7 make the taxonomy complete for RELATIONSHIPS. Compounds are not overlaps — they live at 6. Relationships that transcend gift exchange live at 7.

Correlation with the Collaboration Spectrum (10-stage, sibling file collaboration_spectrum_holomap.md): the taxonomy is a MENU (what can pass between two people); the Spectrum is a LADDER (how much structure the relationship carries). Gifts 1-5 can occur at any Spectrum stage, but frequency and bidirectionality rise as you climb. Aspect 6 (Weave) corresponds to Spectrum stages ~4-7 (Collabs → Teams). Aspect 7 (Communion) corresponds to Spectrum stages 8-10 (Swarms → Holonic Ecosystems → Mycelium Symbiosis). Early stages: a single gift is an event. Late stages: gifts are ambient.

## ❤️ Essence

The right introduction is never one thing. It's a specific gift, given at a specific moment, in a language the receiver can use. Sometimes it's a mirror. Sometimes it's a door. The rightness isn't luck — it's a match between what one person has to give and what the other actually needs right then.

## 🧠 Significance

This is the gift taxonomy and the needs taxonomy at once — a gift received is a need met, just seen from the other side. That double reading matters: it gives the Proactive Matchmaker one shared type system for both halves of every match — what I seek, crossed with what I naturally give.

## 🔥 Consequences

Match proposals get 10x more concrete when they name the gift: "Marina can likely show you your blind spot on distribution" beats "you two could co-learn." Accept/decline per gift-type becomes the vocabulary the learning loop trains on. This taxonomy is used by the Proactive Matchmaker v1 spec (`docs/specs/proactive-matchmaker/`).
