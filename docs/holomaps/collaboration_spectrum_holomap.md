# Collaboration Spectrum Holomap

**Spectrum of Holonic Collaboration Readiness / Evolution — v2.6**

Sasha's original, co-created with AI using integrative approaches. Source PDF in `assets/`.

The evolutionary stages are not linear — they are spiralic. We loop through them at new octaves for each creation.

## The map

| Stage | Name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Holding Fortress | Operating in isolation, guarding knowledge, avoiding "competitors." |
| 2 | Random Conversations | Casual conversations begin, trust starts to glimmer. |
| 3 | Exchanges | Swapping tools, ideas, and best practices — an open-source curiosity. |
| 4 | Collabs | One-off shared projects with aligned allies; revenue-shared but still compartmental. |
| 5 | Partnerships | Ongoing duo collaborations with shared vision and operational rhythm. |
| 6 | Gigs | Multi-party one-off gatherings: curated summits, hackathons, gigs. |
| 7 | Teams | Recurring co-creation among diverse players: a rhythm of trust, roles, and ritual. |
| 8 | Swarms | A living ecosystem of teams, flexibly interwoven like a DAO, studio, or hive. |
| 9 | Holonic Ecosystems | Holonic teams that plug into a planetary lattice — fluid, sovereign, synergistic. |
| 10 | Mycelium-like Collective Symbiosis | Silent symphony of all layers connected, real-time weaving, and co-evolving in conscious collaboration with AI, Earth, and Source. |

## ❤️ Essence

This map tracks one thing: how ready a person or group is to collaborate, and what collaboration looks like at each level of readiness. It starts at isolation (Stage 1, guarding your own knowledge) and ends at full symbiosis (Stage 10, everyone and everything co-evolving together). Each stage is a real, recognizable mode people actually operate in — not an aspiration list.

## 🧠 Significance

Most people and orgs sit somewhere in the middle of this spectrum without naming it. Naming the stage tells you what's actually possible right now — you can't run a Stage 8 swarm with people still operating from Stage 1 fortress-holding. It also gives a diagnostic: if a collaboration keeps failing, check whether the parties are even at the same stage before blaming the idea or the tool.

## 🔥 Consequences

The platform's matchmaker function exists to move people up this spectrum — from isolated (1-3) toward team and swarm (7-8) and beyond. Stages 8-10 correspond to the "Communion" octave of the Collaboration Gift Taxonomy (see sibling file `collaboration_gift_taxonomy_holomap.md`). Every match the Proactive Matchmaker makes is, structurally, a bet on moving someone one stage forward — not just filling a task.
