## The Holonic Levels

```
Individual → Team → Land-based Community → Bioregion → Planet
   (part)   (whole/part)   (whole/part)      (whole/part)   (whole)
```

Each level is **both a whole unto itself AND a part of something larger**.

| Level | What It Is | Examples |
|-------|------------|----------|
| **Individual** | A whole person with their own genius, mission, assets | You, me, each user |
| **Team** | Small coordinated group with shared purpose | Coaching pod, project team, mastermind |
| **Land-based Community** | Physical community with shared space | Ecovillage, intentional community, retreat center |
| **Bioregion** | Network of communities in a geographic area | Cascadia, Costa Rica South, Bali hub |
| **Planet** | The whole system | All of us |

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. The Individual

**Who they are:**
- A person seeking transformation, growth, or purpose
- Often overwhelmed by options; doesn't know where to start
- Wants to contribute but doesn't know their unique gifts
- Seeks a path, not just content

**What they get:**

| Dimension | What They Learn |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Identity** | Who am I? (Archetype, talents, intelligences, types) |
| **Vitality** | Where am I? (8-domain quality of life snapshot) |
| **Capital** | What do I have? (6-tier asset map with strategic leverage) |
| **Purpose** | Where am I going? (Mission from pillar to action) |
| **Action** | What's my next move? (Personalized, matched to weak spots) |
| **Growth** | Am I evolving? (XP, levels, growth paths, streaks) |

**Core message:**
> "A mirror, a map, and a game."

---

### 2. The Community Leader / Facilitator

**Who they are:**
- Runs a community, cohort, or collective focused on transformation
- May be a coach, facilitator, teacher, or organizer
- Feels the burden of tracking member growth manually
- Wants members to self-organize while staying aligned to mission

**What they get:**

| Capability | Benefit |
|------------|---------|
| Instant genius profiling | Know every member's unique gifts (AI-first = zero friction) |
| Growth path visibility | Members walk their own path; leader sees the landscape |
| Mission alignment dashboard | See who's committed to which cause |
| Asset map | Know the collective capital your community holds |
| Daily engagement engine | Members return because the loop is eudaimonically designed |
| Marketplace infrastructure | Creators monetize their genius inside the community |

**Core message:**
> "A dashboard for collective evolution."

---

### 3. The Bioregional / Network Coordinator (Future)

**Who they are:**
- Coordinates multiple communities in a region or network
- Needs visibility across communities without micromanaging
- Interested in emergent coordination and resource flow

**What they get (planned):**
- Aggregated dashboards across communities
- Cross-community mission alignment
- Shared asset pools and collaboration matching
- Network-wide vitality metrics
