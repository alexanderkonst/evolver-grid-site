## Apple Seed Technology

### What It Is
The highest-accuracy "scanner" for human uniqueness. A technology to articulate someone's Zone of Genius with maximum signal-to-noise ratio.

### Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Taxonomy** | 81 talents built on Enneagram types, written for workplace applicability |
| **Assessment** | Extracts top 3 talents (99th, 98th, 97th percentile) |
| **Prompt to AI** | Template + perspectives + high-accuracy samples |
| **Perspectives** | Archetype, Mastery Action, Roles/Functions |
| **Samples** | 2-3 high-accuracy examples (user, Karime, others) |

### Output Format (Compact, Not 30 Pages)

- One-sentence **archetype**
- One-sentence **mastery action** (the one thing that leads to mastery)
- One paragraph on **roles and functions**

### Why It's Frontier

- Most assessments give 30-page reports with no action
- Zone of Genius is the #1 thing AI models say is most useful for personalization
- Combines: Enneagram + Jungian + Developmental + Integral Theory + experiential knowing

---

## Excalibur Technology

### What It Is
Takes the articulated genius and makes it USEFUL. Pulls the sword from the stone.

### The Problem It Solves
Most people who articulate their genius do nothing with it. The sword stays in the stone.

### Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Perspectives** | Template of aspects for usefulness |
| **Unique Offering** | Who is ideal client? What's the A→B transformation? |
| **Samples** | High-accuracy examples of genius → offering conversion |

### Output Format

- **Ideal Client Profile** (who needs this genius)
- **Transformational Promise** (the A → B they deliver)
- **Unique Offering** (the package of value)

---

## Destiny Technology

### What It Is
Converts the useful genius into an actual business with actual sales.

### The Philosophy
If the signal is maintained accurately through all three steps, the resulting business is INEVITABLE. Anyone working on this person's genius monetization would arrive at the same core structure.

### Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Business Architecture** | How to package and deliver the offering |
| **Monetization Pathway** | Pricing, positioning, sales |
| **Signal Verification** | Litmus test: does the business still smell like the genius? |

## How AI Fits In

### The Prompt Structure

1. **Context:** Explanation of the ontology (like the voice note above)
2. **Template:** The perspectives to analyze
3. **Samples:** 2-3 high-accuracy examples
4. **Hypothesis:** AI gets inspired by seeing what high accuracy looks like

### Why It Works

- AI already knows a lot about the person (if relationship exists)
- The prompt provides the LENS through which to see
- The samples calibrate the AI's output format and depth

### For Those Without AI Relationship

Offer the assessment → provides the signal that AI can then work with.

## Terminology: Energetic Naming Principle

> **Design Principle:** Avoid brand names as primary labels. Use energetic essence descriptions.

**Why:**
- Brand names are containers with different meanings per person
- Language creates false shared understanding
- Energetic descriptions capture the essence without boxing it

**Examples:**

| ❌ Brand Name | ✅ Energetic Essence |
|--------------|---------------------|
| Apple Seed | "Articulate your genius" |
| Excalibur | "Make your genius useful" |
| Destiny | "Monetize your genius" |

**Inspiration:** Soviet abbreviations — pointing to shared meaning without assuming it.

## Minimal Viable Genius Business (MVGB)

Definition: The smallest possible business structure that allows someone to monetize their Zone of Genius with paying clients.

Components:
- Unique Offering (from Excalibur)
- One productized service
- One way to collect payment
- One ideal client who pays

---

## Number of Samples

**Recommendation:** 2-3 samples is optimal.
- 1 may not calibrate well
- 3+ adds context length without proportional accuracy gain
- User's own sample + Karime's = strong foundation

---

## The Litmus Test

At every step, ask:

> "Does this still smell like the original signal?"

If yes → proceed.
If no → recalibrate. Noise has entered.
