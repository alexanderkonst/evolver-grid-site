## The "Made for Me" Effect

When something feels designed specifically for YOU:
- You trust it more
- You use it more
- You share it more
- You pay for it
- You become loyal

This is why Netflix, Spotify, TikTok are addictive — they FEEL personal.

**How this achieves it:**
- Zone of Genius Test → "This GETS me"
- QoL Assessment → "This SEES my life"
- Personalized recommendations → "This KNOWS what I need"
- Unique growth path → "This is MY journey"

## Why Clear Paths Matter

**The psychology:**
- Overwhelm = paralysis (too many options = no action)
- Scarcity = action (one next move = done)
- Clarity = confidence ("I know where I'm going")
- Progress visibility = momentum ("I can see I'm moving")

**The existential:**
- Most people feel stuck because they can't see options
- Knowing the path is 80% of taking it
- Transformation happens when the next step is obvious
