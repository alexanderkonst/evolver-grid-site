# Micro-Module Experience & Integration Templates

> Lightweight Experience + Integration sections for all 31 modules

---

## Format

Each module gets:
- **Experience (1-2 min):** One simple action to try now
- **Integration (7 days):** A micro-practice to embed the learning

---

## Foundational Modules (1.4-1.10)

### 1.4: The Five Paths of Growth

**Experience (2 min):**
Rate yourself 1-10 on each path right now: Body, Emotions, Mind, Genius, Spirit. Which is highest? Lowest? Just notice.

**Integration (7 days):**
Daily prompt: "Which path did I nurture today?" Track in notes.

---

### 1.5-1.9: Path Definitions

These are conceptual. No separate Experience/Integration — they prepare for path-specific modules.

---

### 1.10: Balanced Growth

**Experience (1 min):**
Look at your 5-path ratings from 1.4. Pick the lowest. Commit to one tiny action for that path this week.

**Integration (7 days):**
Daily prompt: "Did I do something for my weakest path today?"

**Experience (1 min):**

## Mind Path (M1-M5)

**Integration (7 days):**

**Experience (1 min):**

**Integration (7 days):**

## Emotions Path (E1-E5)

### E1: What Are Emotions?

**Experience (1 min):**
Rate your "emotional slider" intensity right now 1-10. Notice if you've been avoiding certain feelings.

**Integration (7 days):**
Daily prompt: "Did I fully feel something today, or did I push it away?"

---

### E2: Emotional Patterns and Origins

**Experience (2 min):**
Think of a recent strong reaction. Ask: "When did I first learn this pattern?" Childhood? Teenager?

**Integration (7 days):**
Journal once: "A protective pattern I'm still running from childhood."

---

### E3: Processing Emotions

**Experience (2 min):**
If feeling something now: Pause → Locate → Allow → Stay → Breathe → Wait. Try it.

**Integration (7 days):**
Once daily when triggered: run the 7-step protocol.

---

### E4: Working with Intense Emotions

**Experience (1 min):**
Practice anchoring: Feel your feet. Feel the chair. Feel your breath. This is your anchor.

**Integration (7 days):**
Before any potentially intense situation: establish your anchor first.

---

### E5: Emotional Mastery

**Experience (1 min):**
Check: Am I defending against anything right now? Am I wearing a mask?

**Integration (7 days):**
Daily prompt: "Did I show up fully authentic today, or did I hide?"

**Experience (1 min):**
Rate your vitality right now 1-10. What's affecting it most: sleep, food, movement, or stress?

**Integration (7 days):**
Daily vitality check: rate 1-10 each morning.

**Integration (7 days):**

---

### B3: Reading Your Body

**Experience (1 min):**
Body scan: Where's the tension? Shoulders? Jaw? Stomach? Just notice.

**Integration (7 days):**
3x daily: pause and check in with your body. What's it telling you?

## Genius Path (G1-G6)

### G1: What is Genius?

**Experience (1 min):**
What's one thing you do that feels effortless and valuable? Just notice.

**Integration (7 days):**
Ask 3 people: "What do you most value about me?" Collect answers.

---

### G2: Discovering Your Zone of Genius

**Experience (2 min):**
List 3 things you do well. For each: Does it give me energy or drain me? The one that gives energy = closer to genius.

**Integration (7 days):**
Track: "What gave me energy today? What drained me?"

---

### G3: Articulating Your Genius Offer

**Experience (2 min):**
First draft: "I help [WHO] achieve [WHAT] through [HOW]." Write it now, imperfect is fine.

**Integration (7 days):**
Refine your statement. Say it to 3 people. Get feedback.

---

### G4: The Masks We Wear

**Experience (1 min):**
Which mask do you most often wear: Pleaser, Achiever, Helper, Expert, or Rebel?

**Integration (7 days):**
Notice when your primary mask activates. What's underneath?

---

### G5: Authentic Expression

**Experience (1 min):**
What's one small truth you haven't said today? Could you say it?

**Integration (7 days):**
Daily: say one small truth you would normally hold back.

---

### G6: Genius in Full Expression

**Experience (1 min):**
Imagine your genius fully expressed in 5 years. What does that look like?

**Integration (7 days):**
Daily: take one action from your Zone of Genius.
