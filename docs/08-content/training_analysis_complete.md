## Training 3: Zone of Genius

### Practical Use of Zone of Genius:
1. Communicate to world (jobs, roles)
2. Competences grow faster
3. Productive output grows
4. Access Flow State more
5. Monetize creations
6. Feel more excitement
7. Gain clarity and direction

### Purpose vs Mission:
| | Purpose | Mission |
|--|---------|---------|
| **Aspect** | Feminine (Being) | Masculine (Doing) |
| **Definition** | Intrinsic quality/essence within you | Practical application of that essence |
| **Simple word** | Matching ROLES | Matching TASKS |

**Key insight:** Purpose fuels Mission. Mission embodies Purpose.

### Map of Uniqueness Development (5 stages):

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Unawareness — not asking what I'm good at |
| 2 | Initial exploration — figuring out for jobs |
| 3 | Initiation — valuing talents, accepting weaknesses |
| 4 | Integration — finding where talents fit in world |
| 5 | Embodiment — authentic self-expression, maximum impact |

### Zone of Genius Articulation Process:
1. Taxonomy of 81 talents (based on Enneagram)
2. Select top 10 that excite you
3. Narrow to top 3
4. Put into one paragraph/sentence
5. Identify sequence

### Zone of Genius Activation:
- Meditation to recall episodes of being in ZoG
- Feel the sensation in body
- Describe the feeling
- Words = approximation, Feelings = truth

### Application:
1. **Creativity** — AI personalization (the mega-prompt)
2. **Mastery** — Deepening competences
3. **Monetization** — Dream job blueprint

### Monetization paths:
1. Dream job (design job description)
2. Collaborations, gigs, consulting
3. 1-on-1 individual work
4. Workshops (1 to groups)
