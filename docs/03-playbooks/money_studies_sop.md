## Why this exists

Money is the least mapped domain in the corpus relative to its leverage. The platform articulates uniqueness; the roadmap tracks revenue; but there is no holomap of money itself, no digested body of money wisdom, no practice layer. This SOP closes that gap. It is a phase shift technology candidate: the seeing instrument here is *money as a holon*, navigable like any other system, instead of money as an opaque force to be chased or feared.

## The three streams

Every source that enters this layer is read for three distinct yields. They are not the same and must not be collapsed:

1. **Art** — the aesthetic and mythic dimension of money. Images, stories, archetypes, beauty. How money *appears* in culture and psyche. Feeds copy, brand, and transmission.
2. **Wisdom** — the structural truths. How money actually works: creation, flow, storage, exchange, energetics, developmental stages of relating to it. Feeds strategy and the holomaps.
3. **Practice** — what one *does*. Repeatable moves, habits, protocols, experiments with feedback loops. Feeds the roadmap and Sasha's daily operating.

A source may be rich in one stream and empty in another. Name the yield honestly; do not force all three from every source.

## The four quarters of the SOP

The SOP itself is a holon. Four phases, mapped to the quadrants, run as a cycle per source (or per batch of sources):

### I. IDENTIFICATION (It — the object)

*What is this, and is it worth digesting?*

- Sasha hands me books and references; I may also propose sources, flagged as proposals.
- For each candidate, produce a 3-line triage: (1) what body of work it belongs to, (2) which streams it feeds (art / wisdom / practice), (3) leverage estimate for Sasha's current stage (read roadmap + holomap state first).
- Kill fast. A source that only repeats what the corpus already holds gets one pointer line, not a study.

### II. DEVELOPMENT (Its — the system)

*Build the maps.*

Two holomap types, both following corpus conventions (taxonomy on x, evolutionary stages on y, current position marked `►` where applicable):

**A. The Money Holomap** (one living document, appended over time)
- x-axis: money's interdependent modules, e.g. Creation · Flow · Storage · Exchange · Price/Value · Debt/Trust · Power · Meaning. Refine the taxonomy as sources teach us; changes are dated addenda, not rewrites.
- y-axis: 7 evolutionary stages, from survival-scarcity through transactional, accumulative, strategic, generative, stewardship, to money-as-love/energy (exact stage names crystallize from the sources; do not lock prematurely).
- Each source studied adds dated entries to cells it illuminates, with citation.

**B. Body-of-Work Holomaps** (one per major source or author)
- x-axis: the work's own core modules (its native taxonomy, decoded not imposed).
- y-axis: depth layers: Art (myth/image) · Wisdom (structure) · Practice (protocol).
- Plus: where this work sits relative to other mapped works, and its blind spots (every body of work has shadows; name them).

### III. DIGESTION (I — the interior)

*What does this change in Sasha?*

- Cross the source against Sasha's actuals: his unique business canvas, current revenue reality, his relationship to money as visible in the corpus and sessions.
- Output per source: **1 to 3 digested insights maximum**, each in the form: *seeing → consequence → next developmental step*. Not summaries. Shifts.
- Flag when a source's medicine contradicts Sasha's current operating pattern. That friction is the value; do not smooth it.

### IV. ENRICHMENT (We — the shared field)

*How does it feed the whole?*

- Fold digested material outward: candidate lines for the playbooks, the value ladder, client canvases, funnel copy, workshop material. Money wisdom that stays private is half-alive; this layer exists to be transmitted through the products.
- Propose (never auto-execute) roadmap items and practice experiments with success signals.
- Close each cycle with 1-2 lines for the session log.

## Where things live

```
docs/05-reference/money-studies/
  money_holomap.md            ← the one living money holomap (type A)
  sources/
    <author_or_work>.md       ← one body-of-work holomap per source (type B)
docs/03-playbooks/money_studies_sop.md   ← this file
```

No parallel compressions: digested insights that belong in an existing canonical doc go *into that doc* as dated additions, with a pointer from the money holomap.

## The upgrade loop

The point is not a library. The point is that with each cycle:

1. The **money holomap** gets denser (the map improves),
2. Sasha's **practice** gets one concrete experiment (the founder improves),
3. The **products** inherit the language (the transmission improves).

If a cycle produces maps but no practice experiment and no product candidate, the digestion failed; redo phase III before filing.

## Standing rules

- Definition of Done table before each source study begins.
- Quote sources precisely; never paraphrase a locked formulation.
- Stage-honesty: when a source operates above or below Sasha's current money stage, say so. Medicine mismatched to stage is poison.
- One source at a time reaches DONE before the next opens, unless Sasha batches explicitly.
