**Volume:** 10-15 people
**Energy:** Personal, warm, gift energy — NOT pitch

| Response Type | Your Move |
|--------------|-----------|
| "This is amazing!" | "Thank you ❤️ — if anyone comes to mind who's in this spot, I'd love an intro" |
| "Tell me more" | Share 1-2 testimonial screenshots + the 4-min video link |
| "I might be interested for myself" | "Let's do a free 15-min clarity call — [cal.com link]" |
| "Not right now" | "All good. The page is there whenever the timing is right." |
| No response | Don't follow up for at least 7 days. Then a single soft touch: "Hey — just checking this landed. No rush." |

# Campaign B: The Upstream Partnership (This Week)

**Segment:** 1-2 — Uniqueness workers + Ecosystem holders
**Channel:** LinkedIn DMs (personalized)
**Volume:** 5-8 people
**Energy:** Peer-to-peer, strategic, "your clients' now-what"

## Template B1: Segment 1 — Uniqueness Workers

```
Hey [Name] —

I've been following your work in [purpose coaching / uniqueness discovery / etc.] and I wanted to reach out because I think our work is structurally complementary.

I've built a system that takes people who already know WHO they are — but can't figure out WHAT to build from it — and turns their invisible genius into a structured business. In a single 90-minute session. Live, with AI.

Here's the page: https://aleksandrkonstantinov.com/ignite
And here's what came out for one of the founders I worked with: [founder page]

The reason I'm writing you specifically: your clients hit a natural transition point. They've discovered their uniqueness — then what? That "now what" is exactly where I pick up.

I'm exploring a simple partnership: when someone in your world reaches that point, you send them my way. Revenue share, zero risk for them, zero effort from you.

No pressure at all. If this lands, I'd love 15 minutes to talk it through. If not — I admire your work regardless and I'm glad to be in the same space.
```

## Template B2: Segment 2 — Ecosystem Holders

```
Hey [Name] —

I've been watching what you're building with [their community/network/school] and I see something we could do together.

I've created a methodology that takes overqualified people and crystallizes their unique genius into a complete business — in one 90-minute session. It works reliably and the outputs compound.

Here's the system: https://aleksandrkonstantinov.com/ignite

The connection: your community members inevitably reach a moment where they need something concrete — "I know what I'm about, now how do I build something from it?" That's my exact entry point.

Two things I could offer:
1. **Referral partnership** — send people my way when they're ready, revenue share on outcomes
2. **Custom module** — I can build a version of this specifically for your community's language and context

Would you be open to a 15-minute conversation to see if there's a fit? No sales energy, just two builders comparing notes.
```
