# Product Onboarding Playbook

*Created Day 130 (July 20, 2026), prompted by the Practitioner Node: the platform now begets products by bundling shipped modules, and there was no standard path from idea to product. This is that path.*

The seven steps (each step gates the next):

1. **Name the transformational result** in one sentence a stranger repeats correctly. No result sentence, no product. This is the same discipline as the integrated workflow's **Master Result** and its Transformative-Result Pattern (before-screen names the result, after-screen confirms it happened): *"a toast is not a transformative-result delivery."* Get the sentence right here, or the before/after screens downstream inherit the fog.
2. **Bundle check**: list which ALREADY-SHIPPED modules compose it. New-build required for v1 = not this playbook; the idea exits to the **Integrated Product Building Workflow** instead (see below) and returns here at step 4 once the module ships.
3. **Underwrite the category** (Domain 105): what vacant category does this occupy; who else could claim it; why us.
4. **Terms-first draft** (Domain 110): ownership, rev-share, exit rights on one page BEFORE any offer conversation.
5. **First-instance proposal**: sell it to ONE named person before any landing page exists (BIG IDEA #1: the first holon tests everything). The proposal text is written for that person by name. Mirrors the workflow's **"The Bridge: Pain + Promise → 1st Session"** — sell and deliver to one person before productizing.
   - **Roast gate**: before sending, roast the proposal 3 cycles (clarity / honesty / what-was-missed), mirroring the workflow's roast discipline.
6. **Landing from the accepted proposal**: the landing page is the accepted proposal, generalized. Not the reverse. This is the Product Path's output crossing into the workflow's **Market Path playbooks table** (Marketing / Distribution / Brandbook).
7. **Shelf it**: /products entry + value-ladder placement + docs/04-products/ spec doc + roadmap line. A product that is not on the shelf does not exist (Domain 103).

## Relationship to the Integrated Product Building Workflow

This playbook governs the COMMERCIAL birth of a product: idea → first sale → shelf. `docs/03-playbooks/integrated_product_building_workflow.md` governs the BUILD execution of a module: spec → architecture → UI → code. The fork is Step 2 (Bundle check): a **bundle-product** (composed entirely of already-shipped modules, sold before anything more is built) stays on this playbook straight through to Step 7. A product that needs **new build** for v1 exits here to the integrated workflow — 5-element INPUT, progress tracker, roast gates and all — and re-enters this playbook at Step 4 once the module ships and can be bundled.
