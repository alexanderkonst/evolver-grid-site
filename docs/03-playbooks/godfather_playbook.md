## PART 1: QUIZ REDESIGN (Phase 2 — after validation)

> ⚠️ The current ZoG quiz uses a prompt→AI flow. Redesigning into a fixed-question quiz
> is a multi-day engineering project. Document now, build after first sales validate.

### Core Shift
Most quizzes classify. Ours should **expose a tension they can't ignore.**

### New Title
"Why is it still so hard to explain what you do?" ← **DONE (deployed)**

### Proposed 6 Questions (for future fixed-question version)

**Q1 — Identity friction**
When someone asks what you do, what happens?
- I explain it differently every time
- I over-explain and lose them
- I simplify too much and it feels wrong
- I avoid the question entirely

**Q2 — External validation mismatch**
What do people consistently come to you for?
- Advice / clarity
- Strategy / direction
- Solving complex problems
- Something hard to describe

**Q3 — Internal tension (KEY)**
Which feels most true?
- I'm more capable than my results show
- I know what I do, but can't package it
- I've tried to define it, nothing sticks
- I keep looping between ideas

**Q4 — Money distortion**
Which situation are you closest to?
- Undercharging
- Giving value for free
- Inconsistent income
- Avoiding selling altogether

**Q5 — Failed attempts**
What have you already tried?
- Coaching / programs
- Branding / positioning
- Building something (didn't stick)
- Thinking about going back to something stable

**Q6 — Readiness (filters buyers)**
If this became clear, what would you do?
- Act immediately
- Start reaching out to people
- Adjust my current work
- Think about it for a while

### Outcome Archetypes
- **The Multi-Talent Trap** — too many skills, no container
- **The Invisible Genius** — delivering results nobody can name
- **The Misaligned Vector** — applying force in wrong direction
- **The Underpriced Operator** — real value, broken pricing

### Result Logic (every outcome must say)
1. "This is you"
2. "This is why it hasn't worked"
3. "This is incomplete"

## PART 2: TRAFFIC STRATEGY

### Principle
> You're not in a volume game. You're in a precision resonance game.
> You need 20–30 right people, not 10,000 random ones.

### Channel 1 — Direct Signal (HIGH CONVERSION)

**Platforms:** WhatsApp, Telegram, Twitter DMs, Email, LinkedIn

**Message structure:**

```
I built something that shows you what you actually do
in a way that's hard to see from the inside.

Curious if it lands for you.

👉 [link to quiz or landing page]
```

**Rules:**
- Curiosity > pitch
- Personal > broadcast
- No pressure

### Channel 2 — Signal Posts (3 posts this week)

**Post 1 — Identity Hook**
```
You're not confused.
You're just unstructured.

Most people trying to "find their niche" already have it.
They just can't say it in a way that sells.

👉 [link]
```

**Post 2 — Pain Mirror**
```
"I'm more capable than my results show"

That sentence is the whole problem.

Not mindset. Not discipline.
Structure.

👉 [link]
```

**Post 3 — Authority**
```
I've sat with hundreds of people who couldn't explain what they do.

In 90 minutes, it becomes obvious.

Not because we discover something new.

Because we finally name what's already there.

👉 [link]
```

### Channel 3 — Conversion Responses

When someone reacts to a post or message:

```
Want me to take a look at what you got?
```

or

```
If it resonates, the session is where it actually gets finished.
```

**NEVER pitch. NEVER explain. Just open the door.**
