Coaches, guides, and transformation workers often fall into quicksand:
- Sound utterly vague
- Struggle with selling
- Offer "transformation" (which sounds like work, commitment, discomfort)

**Bitter truth:** People don't buy what they NEED. They buy what they WANT.

| What They Want | What They'll Avoid |
|----------------|-------------------|

## The Lean Asset Formula

One page or short video that:

1. **States the pain** in their own words
2. **Names the concrete outcome** (3D, measurable)
3. **Shows 1-2 proof points** (authority without bragging)
4. **Presents with refined aesthetic quality**
5. **Ends with sovereign, no-neediness invitation**
