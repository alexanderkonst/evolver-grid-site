## What it is

Equilibrium is one scrollable page. Two modes:

- **ATTUNE** — read the cycles. You do not fill anything in here (except Moon Focus, once a cycle — see below).
- **ACT** — five inputs you complete *as clarity arrives across the lunar month*, not in one sitting.

The watch is meant to be **glanced at**. Reading takes seconds. Filling happens phase by phase.

---

## When to start a new fill-out

**Right after the Full Moon — once the harvest has been celebrated.**

The previous lunar cycle closes in two steps:

1. **Harvesting** (Full Moon) — ship what's ripe, cut, receive the fruits of labor.
2. **Celebrating** (Waning Gibbous) — announce the harvest. Thank what made it possible. Feel the gratitude.

Then the wheel turns. From the realized potential of that harvest, the next intention starts to surface. That surfacing is the **Planning** phase (Last Quarter, ~3.5 days after the Full Moon). This is when a new fill begins.

You don't sit down on day one and complete the form. You open the watch through the month and capture what's surfaced.

## ATTUNE — five readings

Top-down, in the order you scroll. Nothing to type. Just read.

### 1. Synthesis Reading

One sentence that compresses everything below into the current moment. Tap (or press `r`) to regenerate.

**If you only look at one thing on the watch, look at this.** It's the "what time is it" for your whole self.

### 2. Solar Energy — your personal year

Anchored to your **birthday**, not the calendar. Your year has five phases:

| Phase | Window | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| Fresh start | birthday + ~2 weeks | New road open. Maximum potential, minimum proof. |
| Big push | ~days 14–90 | The year's biggest output belongs here. |
| Steady stretch | ~days 90–270 | The long middle. Rhythm, not spikes. |
| Harvest time | ~days 270–335 | Reap. Close projects. Announce wins. |
| Wind down | last ~30 days | Resolve loose ends. Don't start big things. |

### 3. Zodiac Energy

The current zodiac sign and its quality. A flavor layer.

### 4. Lunar Energy — the primary reading

**The most important reading on the watch.** The 29.5-day cycle, eight phases. Each phase has one specific job:

| Phase | Astronomical | Job |
|---|---|---|
| Planning | Last Quarter | The next intention surfaces. Notice it. Name it. |
| Planting | Waning Crescent | The 1–3 strategies reveal. Write them down. |
| Clearing | New Moon | Release fear. Cry, don't complain. The how is not yours yet. |
| Gathering | Waxing Crescent | Say yes to whatever arrives. Take meetings. |
| Seeing | First Quarter | The how reveals. Write it down before clarity drifts. |
| Leading | Waxing Gibbous | Prep for harvest. Set up tools, helpers, storage. |
| Harvesting | Full Moon | Reap what's ripe. Ship. Cut. Receive. |
| Celebrating | Waning Gibbous | Announce. Thank. Feel the gratitude. |

Below the active pill the watch shows the **time left in the phase** ("ends Tue May 19 · 2.3 days left"). That turns the moon from a vibe into a window — you can schedule into the remainder.

The eyebrow emoji above the pills (🔥 💧 🌍 🌬️) is the holonic quadrant covering two consecutive phases. Background context; nothing to act on.

**One input inside ATTUNE: Moon Focus.** Below the pills. 1–3 words for *this cycle's intention*. Capture it during the Planning phase, when the intention is surfacing. Don't engineer it — *notice* it.

### 5. Day-of-Week Energy

Seven planetary days. The framing predates the Greeks and Romans and still composes the week:

| Day | Planet | Mode |
|---|---|---|
| Sun | Sun | Illumination, celebration |
| Mon | Moon | Intuition, reflection |
| Tue | Mars | Action, courage |
| Wed | Mercury | Clarity, communication |
| Thu | Jupiter | Expansion, big-picture |
| Fri | Venus | Beauty, harmony, connection |
| Sat | Saturn | Structure, discipline, closing-out |

The wisdom: **don't run the whole week as one mode.** Honor each day at least a little and the week composes itself.

## ACT — six sections, filled as clarity arrives

The order below matches the lunar fill sequence. If a section is empty, leave it. Clarity has not arrived yet.

### Lifelong Dedication *(rarely changes)*

One sentence. Synced from Mission Discovery. The chosen direction your action keeps taking, at life scale.

→ If empty, do Mission Discovery first.

### Role *(rarely changes)*

One sentence. Synced from ZoG / Top Talent. Your current Top Talent in plain words.

→ If empty, do Top Talent Discovery first.

### Moon Focus → **Planning phase**

1–3 words. The cycle's intention. (Captured in ATTUNE mode under Lunar Energy — same input.)

### Current Strategy → **Planting phase**

1–3 strategies that translate the intention into directions. Capture them as they reveal themselves through Planting (and through Seeing, if late).

The info icon reminds: *set when you have clarity*. If a strategy hasn't shown up, don't force it.

### Workstreams + Intuitive Tasks → **Seeing phase onward**

By First Quarter, the *how* becomes obvious. Write it down before it drifts.

- **Workstreams** — the streams of work the strategies open. Up to 7. Drag to reorder.
- **Intuitive Tasks** — the concrete moves under each workstream. Up to 7 per stream.

### DO NOW → **Harvesting / any execution day**

The chosen action collapses everything above into one move.

- Up to 3 tasks. **1 is recommended.**
- Each task on the Workstream list has a **DO NOW** button — promote it here.
- Check the box → animates complete, cascades back to the workstream's done-pile.

## A normal day on the watch

1. Open the page. ATTUNE first.
2. Read the **Synthesis** sentence.
3. Glance at the **Lunar pill** — which phase, how many days left.
4. Glance at the **day of week** — what mode is native today.
5. Flip to ACT.
6. Look at **DO NOW**. If empty, promote one task from a workstream. If full, do the work and check the box when done.

That's a complete session. The fill-out work happens on Planning / Planting / Seeing days — not every day.

## Three Degrees — what the watch is for

1. **Unconscious of cycles.** *"Things are wrong with me."* You feel the terrain without seeing it.
2. **Conscious of cycles.** *"Oh — today is the New Moon. I'm supposed to feel like this."* Recognition dissolves the shame loop.
3. **Conscious collaboration with cycles.** *"It's a Clearing day — I'll move strategic-clearing work forward and reschedule execution to a Doing day."* You shape the calendar around the watch.

The watch earns its keep at Degree 2 and matures into Degree 3 over weeks.

---

## Rules

- Don't fill ACT in one sitting. Each section has its phase. Fill it then.
- If a section is empty, leave it. Clarity hasn't arrived yet.
- One DO NOW is better than three.
- Don't start new things during Harvesting. Finish what the cycle already grew.
- Don't skip Celebrating. Announce and thank. Gratitude is the emotional fuel for the next Planning.
