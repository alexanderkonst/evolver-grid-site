## Purpose vs Mission — Working Frame (Sasha's distinction, Session 1)

The frame that organized this session, demystified across traditions (Christian mysticism, Indian traditions, Buddhism, the hero's journey, dharma) down to a simple masculine/feminine split:

- **Purpose = feminine = being.** How you uniquely, natively, innocently express. The pattern underneath all the different ways and projects across a life. It does not change (personality is stable relative to others across the lifespan). *Like the purpose of a candle-snuffer is to put out candles; the purpose of each human is different, and that difference is the purpose.*
- **Mission = masculine = doing = direction.** By being who I am, what do I get to do? The directivity, the line a life moves along. *"By being who I am, what do I get to do."*

Uniqueness has always been the source of success: the more authentic and unmasked the person, the more the work lands. Both artifacts below derive from this one root.
