## 1. LinkedIn Post (all 3 layers)

The entire startup industry is a billion-dollar workaround for not knowing yourself.

They teach you to find PMF by outgrinding 99.9% of people through 100s of pivots. Good luck with that.

82% of first-time founders fail. The #1 reason? "No market need."

But here's the thing nobody says out loud:

There WAS a market need. It just wasn't THEIR market need.

The founder picked a market that seemed rational but wasn't an expression of who they actually are. So they couldn't sustain the energy to find the subtle fit that only comes from deep alignment.

**What if you ARE the product-market fit but your self-knowledge is the bottleneck?**

Every iconic company was built by a founder who couldn't NOT build it. Jobs couldn't NOT build Apple. Musk couldn't NOT build Tesla.

The industry says: find the market, then fit yourself to it.
The truth: know yourself, and the market reveals itself.

**YOU ARE THE PMF.**
Self-knowledge is the bottleneck.
Get your Founder-Market Fit first. PMF follows.

I'm building something around this. If this hit you — let's talk.
