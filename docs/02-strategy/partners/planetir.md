## 1. Synthesis (one paragraph)

Planetir is a German-stewarded, AI-native "ecosystem platform" for the regenerative / consciousness / changemaker scene. It's a social network plus marketplace plus token-governed commons, built by the team behind Berlin engineering shop Think-it (Joscha Raue, Mehemed Bougsea, Dutch co-founder Daphne Laan), legally seated in non-profit Kulimi e.V. Tagline: *"Empowering Change through Heart and Tech."* Self-description: *"a digital home for people and communities to deepen connection and collaboration, and co-create the world we know is possible."* What's distinctive: they explicitly fuse (a) trust-based matchmaking among "Planeteers" via a "Merlin AI" resonance engine, (b) an immersive offline gathering arm (Tunisia, Sri Lanka, Czechia residencies), and (c) a $PLANETIR token + Collective Fund modeled on RetroPGF. Voice is unusually overt in feminine-leadership and inner-work register. Traction: ~500 users including 140 pilot members across 26 countries, 43% female. North star: 144,000 Planeteers by 2027.

### 2.3 Whitepaper (full text, captured 2026-05-25)

#### Vision, Mission & Approach

> The future needs a new foundation. One built on trust, transparency, connection, and collective wisdom.
>
> We envision a world where people and planet thrive in harmony. A world where we remember our interconnectedness and act as living ecosystems. Where technology and AI amplify our humanity instead of distracting from it — helping us care more deeply, create more courageously, and act more wisely. A world where systems nurture creative expression and community, and where capital, governance, and technology are designed to serve life.
>
> Planetir is an invitation to create this world together. We're a digital home for people and communities to deepen connection and collaboration, and co-create the world we know is possible. Because we're all planetary citizens — united in our shared humanity and strongest when we combine our gifts and talents.

**Three core dimensions** (interconnected layers, not separate pillars):
- **Connection** — Find your people and build meaningful relationships rooted in shared values, vibe, and purpose
- **Learning** — Grow your awareness and capacity to lead inner and outer change through curated experiences and collective wisdom
- **Action** — Realize your vision through opportunities for collaboration, jobs, and funding

> Planetir is more than a platform. It's infrastructure for the commons enabling a new kind of economy — one rooted in life.

#### Future of Learning and Work

Conviction: *"Only by nurturing our inner world can we truly regenerate the outer world."* Cultivate **heart skills** (compassion, resilience, creativity, collaboration).

Frame: the old model of work (stable, linear, hierarchical) is dissolving. Generative AI is rewriting how we learn, create, collaborate. The new questions: *"Who am I, beyond what a résumé can capture? What does it mean to contribute in a world where machines can do so much?"*

> When your path aligns with what truly matters to you — and you bring your unique gifts, talents, and creative energy into what you do — something shifts. Your work life becomes your life's work.

Learning principle: **Each One Teach One.** Lifelong, self-directed, curated.

#### Locality + communities

> Communities, not centralized systems, are becoming the focal point for regeneration and growth. Locality is key to solving global challenges.

Thesis: tap global collective intelligence, channel it into place-based impact.

#### Core Values
- **Regeneration**
- **Transparency & Integrity** (*"Individuals have a right to privacy, organizations don't"*)
- **Healthy Relationships** (Do No Harm, multigenerational)
- **Long-term Stewardship**
- **Equity & Inclusion**

#### $PLANETIR Token Mechanics

**Disclaimer:** "While we draw inspiration from web3 principles, the token mechanism will initially be implemented using a simple web2 points system to ensure prototyping with inclusivity and ease of use."

**Issuance logic** — tokens earned/granted for:
- Foundational contributions (early funders, Stewards, Mission Keepers, Founding Partners)
- Revenue-generating platform activity (booking learning experiences, hiring talent, engaging service contracts)
- Ecosystem infrastructure contributions (tech, governance design, community building)
- Donations or contributions to the Collective Fund
- *Phase 2:* non-transactional contributions (referrals, mentoring, open-source tools, organizing gatherings, culture and storytelling)

**Governance rights** — tokenholders vote on:
- Collective Fund allocation
- Constitution upgrades and amendments
- Committee elections
- Mission-aligned ecosystem decisions

**Voting mechanism:** Quadratic voting (to discourage plutocracy).

**Other:** Token is not publicly tradeable or redeemable. Community-approved discretionary buybacks possible. Profit-sharing points TBD.

**Revenue model (Phase 1):** 10-20% commission on:
- Learning Experiences (retreat/workshop/course bookings)
- Job Matchings (% of first-year salary)
- Service Engagements (% of contract value)

**Token recognition split:** 50/50 between buyer and seller on each transaction.

**Collective Fund allocation:** 10% of each transaction routed to the fund.

#### Governance Structure

1. **Partners & Executive Team** (2025-2030 transition phase) — operational leadership, will sunset into community governance
2. **Mission Keepers** — up to 12 individuals. Symbolic/values-centered body. Elders, youth, subject-matter experts. Have veto power on critical proposals (requires 10 of 12 in alignment). Awareness rights to surface ecosystem issues. Re-elected every 3 years.
3. **Planetir Stewards** — up to 144 early contributors. Strategic bridge between Exec Team and broader community. Approve Constitution updates and major funding proposals. Sole authority over RPGF allocation.
4. **Community Voting** — tokenholders vote on operational matters via quadratic voting.

**Constitution** — guiding framework. Amendments require:
- *Core value amendments:* 66-75% Token House + 66-75% Stewards Circle + 2-week Mission Keeper veto window
- *Other amendments:* simple majority both bodies

**Decentralization roadmap:**
- **Phase A:** establish Token House, Stewards Circle, Mission Keepers Council, initiate RetroPGF
- **Phase B:** reduce dependencies between governance components, enhance grant ROI measurement, align impact with profit
- **Phase C:** self-managing system, governance minimization, anti-plutocracy

#### Collective Fund

Held by a foundation/trust. Seeded by 10% of all ecosystem revenue + voluntary contributions/grants.

Eligible projects:
- Climate-adaptive ventures & purpose-driven startups
- Community-driven ecological regeneration
- Access-to-education efforts
- Open-source tooling and infrastructure
- Grassroots cultural initiatives

Mission Keepers hold veto rights on disbursements above €50,000.

Funded projects invited to commit to voluntary revenue share back into the fund. Self-determined, publicly visible per project.

#### Retroactive Public Goods Funding (RPGF)

Rewards already-completed contributions to the ecosystem.
- Anyone can nominate
- Stewards Circle reviews (Mission Keepers may assist on values alignment)
- Voted via one-person-one-vote
- Recipients receive grants

#### Seed Funding (2025/26)

**Convertible Steward Agreement** — early capital as convertible loan. Cap: up to 4× return in case of profitability. Purpose-aligned, interim vehicle similar to a SAFE. Neither equity nor donation.

## 5. Collaboration angles for findyourtoptalent.com

### Context (for cold readers of this doc)

Find Your Top Talent is an open-source coordination infrastructure for conscious innovation. Built around (a) the 7-artifact Unique Business canvas methodology for articulating unique genius and building ventures from it; (b) AI-powered matchmaking between complementary collaborators; (c) white-label / forkable-per-community as the primary commercial channel (community ecosystem leaders deploy their own branded skin on shared rails); (d) a federated-network thesis where each community is a holon. Founder: Alexander Konstantinov, Mexico City.

### Overlap zones

- **Methodology overlap is high.** Planetir's Resonant Profile + Quest + Offer + Matchmaking quartet is a thinner cousin of the 7-artifact Unique Business Canvas. Both bet on articulated uniqueness as the matching substrate; both use AI ("Merlin" on their side, the canvas + matching engine on ours) to surface fit.
- **Audience overlap is near-total.** Their *"changemakers, entrepreneurs, investors, artists, technologists, visionaries, spaceholders, wisdom keepers"* maps cleanly onto Sasha's target. Their 140-pilot / 26-country / 43%-female base is a pre-warmed pool of exactly the people the FYTT funnel is built for.
- **Holonic / federated thesis is shared.** Their *"community of communities"* and *"one ecosystem, many entry points"* is the same network-of-holons intuition Sasha holds.

### Complementary surfaces

- **FYTT canvas as Planetir depth-layer.** Their profiles + quests + offers are surface artifacts. The 7-artifact Unique Business Canvas would populate those surfaces with much higher precision. Their Merlin AI would have far better matching substrate to work with.
- **Planetir feed as FYTT distribution.** Their live social network of 500 already-warm Planeteers is exactly the audience Sasha's funnel is built for. One foothold, distribution unlocks.
- **Commercial models are non-competitive.** Planetir monetizes transactions (10-20%). FYTT monetizes white-label deployment + sessions. Cross-sell is natural: Planetir community hosts are FYTT white-label prospects; FYTT canvas outputs become Planetir profile/quest content.

### Tensions / non-fits

- **Centralized-first vs. forkable-first.** Planetir is building one platform skin with token governance. FYTT's white-label / forkable-per-community angle is structurally different. Reconciliation paths: (a) Planetir becomes a flagship holon on Planetary OS rails; (b) FYTT plugs into Planetir as the talent-articulation depth layer; (c) FYTT becomes the dev infrastructure Planetir runs on. All possible, all materially different.
- **Heavyweight governance vs. lighter holonic-primitive ethos.** Mission Keepers council + token + Constitution + Berlin legal scaffolding is a lot of structure. Sasha's holonic-primitive build is leaner. Tradeoffs each direction.
- **Both at "show me the money" stage.** Planetir's whitepaper is candid that the token is currently a web2 points stand-in and that significant revenue is aspirational. Sasha's Si–Do (first $555 stranger from funnel) is still unfired. Neither is bringing a finished commercial engine to the table.
- **Vocabulary collision risk.** Both use "Planetary," "ecosystem," "stewards," "regenerative," "infrastructure for the commons." Side-by-side without sharp differentiation reads as brand confusion.
- **Token + Mission Keepers stack would need reconciliation with any federation model.** If Planetir-the-skin runs on FYTT-the-rails, where does $PLANETIR live? Where does the Mission Keepers' veto authority bind? Real architecture conversation, not a marketing one.

### The lead-with move (Sasha's play)

Sasha is building a Planetir-branded skin on FYTT rails. When he walks into the conversation, he can SHOW them their own platform running his coordination infrastructure underneath. That's the demo, the proposal, and the structural-fit argument in one gesture.

This is the perfect embodiment of the Day 77 white-label-as-primary-commercial-channel thesis: *"Each skin should take hours, not days by the second deployment."* The Planetir skin is skin #N+1 after the Network School demo. It proves the play to Planetir AND validates the throughput claim for future ecosystem-leader conversations.

Implications:
- The conversation opens with a **demonstrated** capability, not a pitched one
- Their reaction to seeing their own brand on his rails IS the diagnostic for whether the collaboration is real
- Even if Planetir doesn't move forward, the skin itself becomes proof-of-throughput for the next ecosystem-leader conversation
- If they DO move forward, the proposal already exists in deployable form
