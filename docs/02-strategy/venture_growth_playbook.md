## Part I: PMF Hypothesis Formula

### The Extended Formula

```
üåü If A experiencing E had C, they'd have no more B and get D. üåü
```

| Variable | Meaning | Example |
|----------|---------|---------|
| **A** | One specific person in concrete life situation | "Purpose coach with 3 years experience" |
| **B** | Highest frustration (important, urgent, known to them) | "Can't articulate their unique method" |
| **C** | Absurdly simple solution (one step, one outcome) | "Genius articulation session" |
| **D** | Dream outcome | "Clients come because they GET what you do" |
| **E** | Inner voice in moment of desperation | "I'm supposed to be the expert but I can't explain my own approach" |

### Discovery Sequence

```
A ‚Üí B ‚Üí C (never backwards!)
```

**Most common mistake:** Working on C before A&B are absurdly simple.

## Part II: Step-by-Step PMF Discovery

### Step 1: Write Your Hypotheses

Write down your best current hypotheses using the PMF formula.

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Rookie mistake:** Keeping hypotheses in your head instead of written words. This is psychological defense mechanism avoiding confrontation with real world.

### Step 2: Prioritize

Score each hypothesis 1-10 on:
1. **Urgency** ‚Äî How pressing is this now?
2. **Importance** ‚Äî How significant if solved?
3. **Problem Awareness** ‚Äî Do they know they have this problem?

Use simple table: `| Hypothesis | Urgency | Importance | Awareness | Total |`

### Step 3: Track Changes

Keep older versions of each hypothesis. Evolution is the point.

### Step 4: Improve with AI + Critical Thinking

Iterate until you **can't wait** to go validate and sell.

### Step 5: Problem Interviews

Reach out to potential clients. Interview until initial confusion clears.

### Step 6: Lean Solution

Once pain is validated ‚Üí create leanest possible solution that alleviates maximum pain.

### Step 7: Low-Fidelity Prototype

1. User journey map
2. Screen-by-screen mockup

### Step 8: Pick Business Model & Pricing

(You can change it later)

### Step 9: Solution Interviews ‚Üí SELL

Sales is ultimate PMF signal.

### Step 10: You Have a Seed

Product mockup that sells + growing clients = investors want to talk.

## Part III: Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Fails |
|---------|--------------|
| Working on C before A&B | Solution without grounded pain |
| No clarity on A before B | Fuzzy target, scattered energy |
| Believing C is shortest path | It's actually the longest |
| Vague definitions | No rigor ‚Üí no PMF |
