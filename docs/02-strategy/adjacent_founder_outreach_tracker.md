## Outreach Template

Hi [Name] - I am Sasha, founder of Find Your Top Talent. I am building near the space you are in: self-knowledge, founder clarity, and better collaboration matching. Your work on [Company] caught my eye because [specific reason].

I am not reaching out with a pitch. I would love to compare notes for 20 minutes on what actually makes matching/intros useful in the real world, and where the current tools still break.

## Discovery Call Questions

1. What outcome matters most right now: better matching, faster activation, better follow-through, or monetization?
2. Which signals most reliably predict a great match or introduction today?
3. Where does profile or onboarding data still feel too shallow?
4. What happens after a match, and where does value leak out?
5. Which user segment gets the most surprising value?
6. Where do AI recommendations outperform human intuition, and where do they fail?
7. What trust, privacy, or data-permission friction is hardest?
8. If every user profile could add one structured data layer, what would it be?
9. What do people wrongly assume about this category?
10. Where could an external strategist/operator be genuinely useful: product, partnerships, pilot design, positioning, GTM, or founder support?
