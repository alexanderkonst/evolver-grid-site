## 0. Strategy in one line

One free threshold (the 45-min Direction Call) × threshold-recognition messaging × three segments tested in parallel, falsifiably × two engines launched the same week: cold (LinkedIn DMs) and warm (community webinars with host revenue share) — while the warm base (150 emails) is reactivated first.

## 1. The segment-scoring instrument (playbook upgrade)

Segments are no longer compared in the head. They are scored on 12 axes, 4 holonic groups, 100 weighted points; scores update weekly with real outreach data.

**A. THRESHOLD — interior of the person (30):** A1 Threshold acuteness (10) · A2 Self-recognition in the hook (10) · A3 Motion energy (10)
**B. FIT — the dyad (25):** B1 Drive alignment, Sasha's joy (10) · B2 Method fit (10) · B3 Proof proximity (5)
**C. EXCHANGE — economics (25):** C1 Cultural norm of paying for professional self-investment (10) · C2 Ability to pay (5) · C3 Value ceiling / ascension to BUILT+ (10)
**D. REACH — system (20):** D1 Findability by keyword/channel (10) · D2 Stranger-purchase friction (5) · D3 Decision-cycle speed (5)

Notes: B3 penalizes a 7-month-old business everywhere — tiebreaker, never a gate. B3 and D3 are hypotheses until DM data exists.

## 3. The one free threshold: the Direction Call

- **45 minutes, free.** Named result: *"You leave with your transition named and your strongest next direction on the table. Practically everyone does."*
- **Capacity honesty:** 5 Direction Calls per week, stated openly. Real limit = clean scarcity.
- **Why-free line (canonical):** *"Why free? Because the first 45 minutes is where my work does the most visible good, and it's how I choose who I go deeper with. If we're a fit, I have paid containers and I'll say so in one sentence, not in a pitch."* Sent as message #2 after a connection accepts. Week 1 manual, week 2 automate what was learned.
- **Two-question filter** (before booking): *"So I come prepared and the 45 minutes go entirely into your situation: (1) What's the transition you're in? (2) What have you tried?"* Rationale: minimal pair covering state + motion; Q1 = self-identification at the threshold (answering it starts the work — the filter IS the beginning of the gift); Q2 = filters browsers from movers and reveals the precision gap. A third question adds friction without adding reading. Service framing, never gatekeeping.
- **Funnel monogamy restored:** the 20-min free read is retired; /ignite updated to the Direction Call (shipped Jul 13). One free step system-wide. Ladder: free Direction Call → $555 Productize Yourself session → $1,111 BUILT container.

## 4. Message formula (per DM)

Threshold hook ("transition period" recognition, in harvested client language: limbo, boat in waves, spinning wheels) → recognition line → magnetic story in one line → one CTA (Direction Call). Being-wanted register held: no chasing, no over-giving. One CTA per message. No em-dashes, no "Not X. Y." in external copy.

## 5. Engine 1 — cold: falsifiable LinkedIn tests

- LinkedIn budget: ~100 connection invites/week (safe), note ≤300 chars.
- **3 segments × 30 invites**, equal proportions, one week.
- Pre-registered hypothesis per segment: *"Segment X at 30 touches yields ≥20% accept, ≥15% replies, ≥2 Direction Calls in 7 days."* Miss = hypothesis killed, segment benched, next segment in.
- Metrics chain: accept → reply → booked call → paid. Every DM logged in the tracker. OMTM: offers made per week.
- Campaign template from DAO days: Sasha to retrieve when relevant.

## 6. Engine 2 — warm: community webinars (Group Direction Call)

**Format (~90 min), the 5-step arc:**
1. Philosophy, brief.
2. Everyone creates their profile live on the platform (hands-on — the platform's right role at last).
3. "You now have the words for what you carry into any professional environment" — professional clarity revealing unseen opportunities.
4. Give it to AI → tighter LinkedIn profile recruiters and everyone understand.
5. The reveal: *your professional profile IS your business* — job, side gig, occasional consulting pulls formalized into an offer, paid advice. You were already doing business.
Plus live threshold readings: a few volunteers describe their situation; Sasha names the inner transition and the strategy to the threshold — the live proof segment.

**No free Direction Calls for attendees** — the webinar IS that channel's free step. Next step from the webinar: the $555 session directly.

**Host mechanics:** the community leader posts it as their gift to the community ("emerged from within"); they host/curate questions or just attend first; co-hosting offered; Sasha answers everything. Sasha can bring his own host (Karime, Victoria) if the leader wants zero work.

**Host economics (the pitch):** 33% of the first two purchases per member — $183 per $555 session, $367 per BUILT. Baseline math: 10 attendees/mo, 10% buy a session, a third of buyers ascend → **~$300/month per community for one post, ~$3,600/year.** Assessment: baseline-to-optimistic for a trusted community with live demo; attendance figure is conservative. 33% applies to the first two purchases only — stated upfront.
**Instrument, don't argue:** the predictive metric is profiles-created-live (activation); track attendee → profile → session per community; replace assumed coefficients with real ones after two webinars.
**Falsifiable:** ≥15 attendees → ≥10 profiles created live → ≥1 paid session within 2 weeks.
**Candidate communities:** NS, ClubDAO, Oyi's group (strongest — Oyi produced the first paid stranger), Devon's.
**Sequenced door:** once a host sees the revenue share working, the community onboarding-module purchase becomes a natural conversation (ecosystem holon enters value-first).

### Segment-channel fit (the upgrade)
Findability is channel-specific; each segment gets its best channel, not the same one.
- **Cold LinkedIn DM trio (this week's test):** Fractional executives · US coaches/solopreneurs in limbo (searchable by "coach"; the missing-positioning signal is visible on the profile itself, which is also the pitch) · Big4/MBB consultants at up-or-out.
- **Webinar engine:** conscious web3/tech builders (communities ARE the consciousness filter: ClubDAO, RnDAO) · post-exit founders (trust networks; cold keyword search cannot find "actually free" exits).
- **Deep-research rifle (slow lane):** shortlist of genuinely-free post-exit founders from acquired-company lists; not this week.
- **Wave 3, warm referral only:** pre-university teens (16-17, selective/STEM schools), sold to PARENTS on the профориентация shelf as its category evolution ("direction from his uniqueness, not from fear"). Heinrich's MSU granddaughter = live pilot.

### Cold test protocol (falsifiable, per segment)
1. Pre-registered hypothesis: "Segment X at 30 touches → ≥20% accept, ≥15% reply, ≥2 Direction Calls in 7 days." Miss = killed, benched, next segment in.
2. **Prospect scorecard (before sending, 0-5, send at ≥4):** visible threshold signal (+1) · has built something real (+1) · US/top-5 market (+1) · active on LinkedIn last 30 days (+1) · any warmth (mutual, group, prior touch) (+1).
3. Metrics chain per prospect in tracker: sent → accepted → replied → booked → showed → paid.
4. Message #1 = threshold hook + recognition (≤300 chars in invite note). Message #2 after accept = why-free line + two questions. Manual week 1; automate learned steps week 2.
5. Friday: OMTM count, per-segment funnel, axes re-scored with real data.
