## Purpose

This document catalogs three categories that most people overlook:

1. **Paid Surfaces** — every buyable visibility unit across social, search, retail media, audio, CTV, newsletters, events, and emerging AI assistants
2. **Hidden-in-Plain-Sight Surfaces** — ambient visibility that already exists in everyday workflows (email signatures, device names, document metadata, payment fields, video call layers)
3. **Physical & Physical-Digital Hybrid Surfaces** — body, carry, spaces, public media, and bridges (QR, NFC, wallet passes, beacons, AR)

**Relationship to the Holomap:**
- [Universal Digital Surface Map](./universal_digital_surface_map.md) = what EXISTS (universal inventory)
- [Digital Surface Holomap](./digital_surface_holomap.md) = what MATTERS for Alexander's unique business (scored & sequenced)
- **This document** = the paid and hidden layers that deepen both

# Part I: Paid Surfaces

## Meta Ecosystem (Facebook + Instagram + Threads)

**Formats:** Image/video feed ads, Stories ads, Reels ads, carousel, collection, lead forms, click-to-message (Messenger/DM), Explore placements, Advantage+ distribution, Threads feed ads.

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Min budget** | ~$5–20/day per ad set (practical testing floor) |
| **Targeting** | Strong on first-party + retargeting (pixel + customer lists), algorithmic expansion, demographic/contextual, lookalike modeling |
| **Best for** | Broad awareness → direct response. Lead magnets, booked calls, "message me" funnels, retargeting |
| **Access** | Self-serve (Meta Ads Manager) |
| **Coach/consultant fit** | **High** when paired with lead forms or click-to-message + calendar booking |

## LinkedIn Paid Distribution

Two distinct regimes:

### Campaign Manager (ad impressions/clicks)

**Formats:** Sponsored Content (image, video, carousel), Document Ads, Thought Leader amplification, Lead Gen Forms, Message/Conversation ads, Text/Dynamic variants.

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Min budget** | $10/day minimum (LinkedIn published floor) |
| **Targeting** | **Best B2B targeting available** — job title, seniority, company, industry. Matched Audiences via Insight Tag + list matching |
| **Best for** | High-value B2B leads, webinar registrations, account-based awareness |
| **Coach/consultant fit** | **Very High** when selling to mid-market/enterprise where deal size supports CPC/CPM |

### Sales Navigator (paid outreach)

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Format** | InMail credits — paid messaging to non-connections |
| **Volume** | 50 InMail credits/month (Sales Navigator Core) |
| **Nature** | Functions like "micro-ads" in an inbox. Feels personal |
| **Coach/consultant fit** | **High** — direct outreach to decision-makers |

# Part III: Physical & Physical-Digital Hybrid Surfaces

> *The physical world as a stack of encounter channels: (a) what's on you, (b) what's with you, (c) what's around you, (d) what's in motion with you, and (e) what's institutionalized as media.*

## Surface Attributes Schema

| Attribute | What It Measures |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Who** | Who typically encounters it |
| **Persistence** | One-time, recurring, semi-permanent, permanent |
| **Cost** | Free / low / moderate / high |
| **Effort** | One-time setup, ongoing, per-event |
| **Reach** | 1:1, small group, crowd, mass |
| **Trust** | Low → high ("borrowed trust" is the key multiplier) |
| **Bridge** | How it connects to digital: URL, QR, NFC, SMS, wallet, beacon, AR, audio |

### Two Meta-Notes

1. **Trust asymmetry dominates:** Peer introduction (word-of-mouth) is routinely among the most trusted channels. Nielsen reports very high trust in recommendations from people you know.
2. **Permission boundaries are not optional:** Many "public surface" tactics become illegal or reputationally toxic without consent. Fly-posting, sidewalk stencils, and unauthorized sticking can trigger prosecution.

## Physical-Digital Bridges

> The hybrid zone where physical encounters become measurable funnels. **Rule: make the bridge redundant** (human-readable + machine-readable) because scan behavior varies.

| Bridge Tech | Who | Persistence | Cost | Reach | Trust | How It Works |
|------------|-----|-------------|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| **QR codes** (on any physical surface) | Anyone who can see + scan | Matches host surface | Near-zero | Depends on placement | Inherits host trust | QR → URL (ISO/IEC 18004) |
| **NFC tags** (stickers, cards, plaques) | Smartphone users in close proximity | Long-term if protected | Low | 1:1 → small group per location | Inherits host | NFC tap → URL |
| **Apple App Clips / App Clip Codes** | iPhone users at point of encounter | Durable if printed/installed | Low-mod | Small group → crowd | **High** (native OS pattern) | Scan/NFC → lightweight app experience |
| **Mobile wallet passes** (loyalty/membership/offer) | Opt-in users (high intent) | Long-term (stays in wallet) | Low-mod | 1:1 (high value) | **High** (wallet = trusted UI) | Scan/tap → add pass → notifications |
