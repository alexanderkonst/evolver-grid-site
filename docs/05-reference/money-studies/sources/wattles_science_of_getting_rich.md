**Source:** Wallace D. Wattles, *The Science of Getting Rich* (1910), studied via the Proctor Gallagher Institute workbook edition ("A Lawful Process for the Creation of Wealth," 1996/2007/2014) — Wattles's text + Bob Proctor's stickperson model (conscious / sub-conscious / body, after Thurman Fleet 1934) + per-chapter questions and exercises.

**Lineage position — the genre's DNA at origin:** Brown 1903 → **Wattles 1910** → Hill 1937 → Nightingale/Proctor → *The Secret* (2006, which credits SOGR as its seed) → Eker, Golden, and the whole modern genre. This is the systematized headwater: Brown supplied the inversion; Wattles supplied the *method* ("doing things in a certain way") and the moral architecture.

## Frequency Test Verdict: SPLIT — the cleanest ethics AND the purest belief-engine commands in one text

**What passes at the highest level (the ethics chapters are 111 years ahead of their genre):**

1. **Use value > cash value:** "See that you give them a use value greater than the cash value you are taking from them." This is Golden's buyer-profits-more rule, verbatim in 1910, and STF's reciprocity purity in embryo.
2. **The Impression of Increase:** convey in every transaction, however small, that dealing with you advances the other's life. "You are a creative center from which increase is given off to all." Magnetism through genuine advancement of others — the being-wanted register operationalized.
3. **Creative over competitive plane:** "The mind that seeks for mastery over others is the competitive mind, and the competitive mind is not the creative one." Explicit warning against power over people, domination, and lavish display; commercial kings named as blood-drenched political kings. Anticipates the entire ITFT exclusion list.
4. **"What I want for myself, I want for everybody"** (Golden Rule Jones) named as the finest statement of creative action.
5. **Anti-poverty-worship:** the Right to be Rich chapter grounds wealth in full living of body, mind, and soul; "the expression of love is often frustrated by poverty." Money as substrate for love's expression — one step from Money = Usefulness = Love.

**What fails, explicitly and textually (the belief-engine, in its own words):**

1. **Commanded epistemic closure:** "Do not ask why these things are true, nor speculate as to how they can be true. Simply take them on trust... If a doubt comes to you, cast it aside as a sin. Do not listen to arguments against this idea; do not go to churches or lectures where a contrary concept is taught. Do not read magazines or books which teach a different idea." The immune system of the guru loop, prescribed in plain text at the genre's origin.
2. **Denial of appearances:** "you must hold to the truth that there is no poverty. There is only abundance"; likewise "there is no disease." Spiritual bypass of the LL/LR quadrants elevated to method.
3. **Infallibility claims:** "The combined mental and personal action I have described is infallible. It cannot fail... Getting rich is an exact science" — unfalsifiable by construction; failure can only ever indict the student.
4. **The workbook wrapper is the guru loop industrialized:** Proctor built a fortune teaching this text (Nightingale-Conant → *The Secret* → Proctor Gallagher seminars); the edition's own introduction is a conversion testimony (Sandy Gallagher's seminar-to-partner arc) functioning as funnel copy. The 1910 ethics travel inside a 2014 machine.

**Verdict for corpus use:** quote the ethics chapters (6, 14, 15) freely as first-principles lineage; quarantine the epistemology (ch. 4's trust-commands) as the founding specimen of the belief-engine pathology, cross-referenced to Field Pathologies.

## The Native Taxonomy

1. **The Certain Way** — riches result from doing things in a certain way, not from environment, talent, or thrift; cause and effect, claimed as science.
2. **The First Principle** — one thinking substance; thought in it produces the form imaged; a human is "a thinking center capable of original thought." Three creed propositions to be memorized and held.
3. **Thinking truth regardless of appearances** — "the hardest work in the world"; sustained consecutive thought as the founding labor of wealth.
4. **Creative vs competitive plane** — create, never compete; what you want must not be taken from anyone.
5. **Vision + Faith + Purpose + Gratitude** — the clear mental image held with gratitude and acted toward.
6. **Action in the present** — "Do all the work you can do, every day, and do each piece of work in a perfectly successful manner"; efficient action now, not someday.
7. **The Impression of Increase** — the interpersonal law: every contact leaves the other advanced and feeling it.
8. **The Advancing Person** — the magnetism of visible development; "people will go where they are given increase."

## Depth Layers

### Art
- The **oak tree in the formless substance**: thought as seed, creation along established channels of growth — patience as lawfulness, not delay.
- The **advancing person** radiating increase: the honey-pot image at the level of character rather than stance.
- The **stickperson** (workbook layer): conscious mind chooses, sub-conscious must accept, body executes — a teachable UL diagram (T→F→A→R, which Eker later compresses).

### Wisdom
1. Use value > cash value is the complete ethics of exchange in one sentence.
2. The impression of increase explains organic attraction: people migrate toward whatever demonstrably advances life. (Rhymes with the corpus: every touchpoint IS an act of service.)
3. "Desire is possibility seeking expression" — want as the pressure of unexpressed capacity, not lack (the generative reading of desire; contrast Brown's "brutes want").
4. Thinking against appearances as labor: the honest admission that stance-work is the most expensive work there is.

### Practice
1. Hold the clear vision + gratitude daily; act "in a perfectly successful manner" on today's work only.
2. In every transaction, deliberately put in more use value than cash taken, and *let the other feel it*.
3. The workbook's ch. 14 exercise: name three people who could benefit from your assistance and how you will help each within 30 days — increase made concrete.
4. Quarantined practice: the trust-commands (do not question, avoid contrary ideas) — documented, not adopted.

## Blind Spots

- LL/LR denial by design ("there is no poverty") — the founding bypass the whole genre inherits.
- Unfalsifiability as a feature; failure structurally indicts the student.
- The competitive/creative split, while ethically clean, waves away real contested resources (attention, shelf space, capital are partly rivalrous).
- Workbook layer inherits all of the above plus the seminar-funnel economics.

## Position Relative to the Field

The genre's constitution: Brown gave the stance (1903), Wattles the method and ethics (1910), and everything after is amplification, fragmentation, or industrialization. Uniquely valuable to the corpus because it contains the split *within one text*: the ethics the corpus keeps (use value > cash value, impression of increase, creative plane) and the epistemology the corpus rejects (commanded trust, denial of appearances, infallibility) — making it the definitive teaching specimen for where the frequency line runs.
