## Core Principle

**Nothing is visible until it's earned.**  
The platform reveals itself progressively as users demonstrate commitment.

## Phase 1: Zone of Genius (Entry)

### What Happens
1. User lands on Zone of Genius wizard
2. Completes the discovery flow (AI-first or manual)
3. **First Aha Moment** â€” Results revealed:
   - Archetype
   - Core talents
   - The One Action (path to mastery)
   - Genius colors (visual identity)

### What's NOT Visible Yet
- No sidebar
- No game world
- No other modules
- Just the ZoG experience

---

## Phase 2: Quality of Life Snapshot

### Transition Logic
> "You know your genius. Now let's map your life in 10 minutes."

### What Happens
1. 8-domain QoL assessment (quick, visual)
2. **Second Aha Moment** â€” QoL snapshot:
   - Strengths and growth opportunities
   - "Your biggest growth opportunity: [Domain]"
   - Visual representation

### What Unlocks
- The Game World (sidebar appears)
- Profile Space (ZoG + QoL visible)
- Transformation Space (available but not yet used)

---

## Phase 3: First Unit of Transformation

### Transition Logic
> "To grow your [Biggest Opportunity], here's your first practice."

### What Happens
1. AI recommends a Growth Path that supports their growth opportunity
2. User completes **first transformational unit** (micro-learning)
3. The video/content explains:
   - What this Growth Path develops
   - **Which QoL domains it supports** *(important link)*
4. **Third Aha Moment** â€” "I just grew"

### What Unlocks
- Progress tracking (XP, level)
- Option to continue transformation OR pivot to Genius Discovery
